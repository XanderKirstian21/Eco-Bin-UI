Welcome to Eco-Bin!

Introducing the "Eco-Bin," a reverse vending machine

(RVM) revolutionizing plastic bottle recycling. The

Eco-Bin incentivizes effortless PET bottle recycling

by providing monetary rewards, encourage

engagement through a leaderboard. Integrated into

established waste management systems, this

innovative system not only enhances recycling

efficiency but also contributes to a sustainable

future by rewarding eco-friendly actions through E-

Wallets like GCash or Maya.

Visit Eco-bin on Dampalit Barangay Hall in M. Sioson St, Malabon City, 1470 Metro Manila
